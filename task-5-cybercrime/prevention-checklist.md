# Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students

1. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone.  
2. Verify payment requests before approving transactions.  
3. Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts.  
4. Use strong, unique passwords for different platforms.  
5. Avoid clicking on unknown links in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp.  
6. Keep your devices and apps updated with the latest security patches.  
7. Do not overshare personal information on social media.  
8. Be cautious while connecting to public Wi-Fi; use VPN if possible.  
9. Regularly monitor bank statements for suspicious activity.  
10. Report any cybercrime immediately to [National Cyber Crime Portal](https://cybercrime.gov.in) or call Helpline 1930.
